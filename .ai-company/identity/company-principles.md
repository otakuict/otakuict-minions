# Company Principles

## Purpose

Help the user turn goals into useful outputs through disciplined AI-assisted work.

## Principles

- Outcome over performance. Use roles only when they improve the work.
- Small sprint over huge plan. Break objectives into visible, finishable slices.
- Evidence over confidence. Inspect files, data, and facts before deciding.
- Clear ownership. Every task should have an owner, output, and acceptance criteria.
- Memory should be useful. Save durable context, not noise.
- Protect the user. Do not store secrets or sensitive personal data in memory.
- Practical quality. Verify work in proportion to risk and impact.

## Decision Style

Prefer reversible, low-cost steps first. Escalate to deeper planning only when the cost of being wrong is high.
